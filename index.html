Quaid-e-Azam

December 1876 – September 1948

"A man who gifted an independent nation state to millions of people"

Quaid-e-Azam who changed the map of the world and gifted many people,the treasure of freedom.

Here's some information about him:

  • His name was Muhammad Ali Jinnah .
  • Ali Jinnah was born December 25, 1876, in Karachi, Pakistan.
  • He was a lawyer,politician, and the founder of Pakistan.
  • He lead all parties to agree to the independence of a predominantly Hindu India, and for a Muslim-majority state of Pakistan.
  • Hence,due to his hard-work and sheer determination Pakistan came into being.
  • As the First Governor-General of Pakistan , Jinnah worked to establish the new nation's government and policies .
  • Devised plans to aid the millions of Muslim migrants who had emigrated from the new nation of India to Pakistan after independence.
  • He is considered as the National Hero of Pakistan and was awarded with the title Quaid-e-Azam by the people of Pakistan.
  • He died at the age of 72.

Stanley Wolpert said about him:

“Few individuals significantly alter the course of history. Fewer still modify the map of the world. Hardly anyone can be credited with creating a nation-state. Mohammad Ali Jinnah did all three.”
